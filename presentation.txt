                Dense Only & 0.773 & 0.900 & 0.947 & \textbf{0.13s} \\

                + gte-multi-reranker & 0.520 & 0.867 & 0.913 & 8.36s \\

                \item[\textcolor{accent}{!}] But latency: 0.13s $\rightarrow$ 23s (\textbf{178x})
            \end{itemize}
        \end{column}

\begin{frame}{Conclusion}
    \vspace{0.2cm}
    
    % Insight 1: Embeddings
    \begin{block}{\textbf{1. Domain Specificity Over General Complexity}}
        Experiments confirm that \textbf{fine-tuned dense embeddings} (Vietnamese-Embedding-v2) significantly outperform complex multilingual hybrid architectures for the Vietnamese legal domain.
    \end{block}
    
    \vspace{0.2cm}
    
    % Insight 2: Architecture
    \begin{block}{\textbf{2. The Trade-off: Efficiency vs. Adaptability}}
        \begin{itemize}
            \item \textbf{Traditional RAG:} Superior speed and raw metrics (Relevancy 0.818).
            \item \textbf{Self-Reflective RAG:} Essential for "human-like" robustness (handling chit-chat, ambiguity), despite the latency cost.
        \end{itemize}
    \end{block}
    
    \vspace{0.2cm}
    
    % Final Summary
    {\textbf{Final Verdict:}}
        ViLeXa demonstrates that the future of autonomous legal assistants lies in a hybrid approach: combining \textbf{high-speed, domain-specific retrieval} with the \textbf{adaptive reasoning} of agentic workflows.
    
\end{frame}
